Believers Unite Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 16, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Believers Unite handles personal information collected through the site.

1. What Information We Collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily submit through the contact form, such as:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Messages or inquiries you send us

2. How We Use This Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Read and Respond to your inquiries
  • Follow up if clarification is needed
We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, mailing lists, or sharing with outside parties.

3. How Information Is Stored

Contact form submissions are stored in a secure private spreadsheet software connected to our form. Access is limited to the site owner for communication purposes only.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. We may share data only if required by law or to protect the platform from misuse.

5. Cookies and Analytics

Believers Unite does not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics tools at this time.

6. Your Rights

You may request that we delete your contact submission or any personal information you previously provided. To do so, contact us through the same form.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. Continued use of the site means you accept any updated terms.

8. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent through the site's contact form.
