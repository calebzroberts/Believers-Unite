Lancaster Church Search FAQ

What is the purpose of Believers Unite?

Believers Unite exists to bring Christians together under the primary doctrines of the faith: a triune God, the deity and resurrection of Jesus Christ, the authority of Scripture, the fall of man, and redemption by grace through faith. By uniting around these truths, the Church is strengthened and encouraged.

How do churches qualify to be listed?

We include churches that affirm the essentials of Christianity — one God in three persons, the deity of Christ, His death and resurrection, salvation through Jesus alone, and a commitment to Scripture over the world’s values.

How can I get involved?

The first step is to find a Bible-believing church near you and commit to the community. Attend, serve, fellowship, and grow with other believers. You can also submit your email to receive updates about the Believers Unite mission.

Are there good churches in Lancaster, PA?

Yes. Lancaster County has many solid, Bible-believing churches. Use the search tool on this page to find congregations near you that can help you grow in your walk with Christ.

Meet Caleb, The Founder

The Spirit of God has been moving and continues to move throughout the Nation and the World in unparalleled ways. This, however, does not mean the Church is free of persecution, encroachment of directly opposing religions, or even slaughter.

What does this mean for us? The last thing we need is internal strife in the Church. (1 Corinthians 1:10) It is time for us to unite as Christians under Christ, sticking to sound doctrine: that Jesus is Lord (John 10:30-38), God is one God with three persons (1 John 5:7), salvation is by grace through faith in Christ alone (John 14:6), and the Bible is inerrant and infallible (2 Timothy 3:16).


Knowing there are so many Christians across the globe who believe these things but may disagree on secondary issues, I sought out to unite true believers in Christ and Bible-believing Churches so we can work together to advance the knowledge of the Gospel of Jesus Christ(Matthew 28:19-20) and combat the spiritual forces of evil in this dark world (Ephesians 6:12). And by God’s grace, this vision and project has gotten to this point today.
