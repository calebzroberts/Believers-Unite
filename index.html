Bringing the Church Together
One Believer at a Time

The goal of Believers Unite is to bring together Churches and Believers in Jesus through one unified service that facilitates in-person connection.

Our Mission: Unite believers and Bible-believing Churches under Christ, providing a way for individuals to get connected in their community and grow their faith.

Meet Caleb, The Founder

It seems that every day the church is becoming more divided, even though we ultimately have the same belief that Jesus Christ is Lord. Following the terrible Charlie Kirk assassination, I have felt led and empowered by the Holy Spirit to create a way for others to also. My prayer is to see this turning into a movement to bring together the Church not only in this area but across the country.
