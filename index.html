Bringing the Church Together
One Believer at a Time

Believers Unite exists to help all believers in Christ find and get involved in a sound, Bible-believing church in his or her area.

Our Mission: Bring together the Body of Christ, helping every believer to get involved in the Christian community and grow in faith.

Meet Caleb, The Founder

The Spirit of God has been moving and continues to move throughout the Nation and the World in unparalleled ways. This, however, does not mean the Church is free of persecution, encroachment of directly opposing religions, or even slaughter. What does this mean for us? The last thing we need is internal strife in the Church. (1 Corinthians 1:10) It is time for us to unite as Christians under Christ, sticking to sound doctrine: that Jesus is Lord (John 10:30-38), God is one God with three persons (1 John 5:7), salvation is by grace through faith in Christ alone (John 14:6), and the Bible is inerrant and infallible (2 Timothy 3:16). Knowing there are so many Christians across the globe who believe these things but may disagree on secondary issues, I sought out to unite true believers in Christ and Bible-believing Churches so we can work together to advance the knowledge of the Gospel of Jesus Christ(Matthew 28:19-20) and combat the spiritual forces of evil in this dark world (Ephesians 6:12). And by God’s grace, this vision and project has gotten to this point today.
