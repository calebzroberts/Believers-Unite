FAQ About Harrisburg Churches

Are there good churches in Harrisburg, PA?

Yes! The south-central area of PA, home to Harrisburg, has plenty of great churches you can get involved in. Just search above to find Churches that are closest to you.

What does it mean for a Church to be Bible-believing?

To be a Bible-believing Church, it must affirm the divine authority of Scripture to our everyday lives and recognize that, while the Bible is up to interpretation within context, the Bible is the full, inerrant Word of God.

How do we choose the Churches in this Harrisburg church directory?

We include Churches that affirm the essentials of Christianity — one God in three persons, the deity of Christ, His death and resurrection, salvation through Jesus alone, and a commitment to Scripture over the world’s values.

What is the purpose of Believers Unite?

Believers Unite exists to bring Christians together under the primary doctrines of the faith: a triune God, the deity and resurrection of Jesus Christ, the authority of Scripture, the fall of man, and redemption by grace through faith. We believe if the Body of Christ can unite under these, great things will happen.

How can I get involved?

The first step is to find a Bible-believing church near you and commit to the community. Attend, serve, fellowship, and grow with other believers. You can also submit your email to receive updates about the Believers Unite mission.
