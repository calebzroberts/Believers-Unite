    Sessions

Who’s behind this idea?

Believers Unite is a project focused on bringing together believers in Christ under the core doctrines of Christianity across Pennsylvania. This Pennsylvania Bible Convention idea is designed to bring together Christians from different areas to fellowship, learn, grow, and unite together under the authority of Jesus Christ. Given enough interest, we believe it can become a real event that will bring together believers in Christ from across the state.
